Day-by-day itinerary

D1Arrival in Bali — Seminyak check-in

Flight KTM→DPS via Kuala Lumpur. Private transfer to your 4★ Seminyak resort. Evening at leisure — sunset at Double Six beach. Welcome dinner included.

D2Seminyak — beach day & couple spa

Morning beach club access. Afternoon 90-minute Balinese couple spa session. Dinner at a beachfront grill (included).

D3Ubud — rice terraces & temples

Transfer to Ubud. Tegallalang rice terraces, Tirta Empul water temple, and the Sacred Monkey Forest with a private guide.

D4Ubud — Mount Batur sunrise (optional) & art villages

Optional 4 AM Mount Batur sunrise trek (add-on). Late morning at leisure; afternoon visit to Celuk and Mas art villages.

D5Nusa Penida day trip

Fast-boat day trip: Kelingking viewpoint, Angel's Billabong, Crystal Bay snorkelling (gear included). Farewell dinner.

D6Departure

Private transfer to DPS for your return flight to Kathmandu via KUL. Fly home with the memories.
